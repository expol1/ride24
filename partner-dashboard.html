Partner Terms of Cooperation

Ride24 operates as an online marketplace platform.

§1 Platform Nature

Ride24 operates as an online marketplace platform that enables customers to search for and reserve vehicle rental services offered by independent rental companies (“Partners”).

Ride24 does not provide vehicle rental services and is not a party to the rental agreement.

The rental agreement is concluded directly between the customer and the Partner.

§2 Partner Account

Ride24 provides the Partner with access to a Partner Account on the Ride24 platform. The Partner is responsible for:

  • maintaining accurate information about vehicles
  • updating prices and availability
  • uploading vehicle descriptions and photos
  • maintaining valid rental terms and policies

The Partner guarantees that all information published on the platform is accurate and lawful.

§3 Vehicle Offers

The Partner may publish vehicle rental offers through the Ride24 Partner Panel. Each offer must include:

  • vehicle class and price list
  • rental conditions
  • pickup location
  • required documents

§4 Reservation Process

Customers may create reservations through the Ride24 platform. After a reservation request is submitted, the Partner receives a reservation notification containing: reservation details, vehicle type, and customer contact data.

The Partner must confirm or reject the reservation within 24 hours. Failure to respond within the required time may result in automatic cancellation of the reservation.

§5 Reservation Confirmation

Once the Partner confirms the reservation: the system generates a Ride24 Voucher and the customer receives the voucher electronically.

The Partner agrees to honor all confirmed vouchers issued through the Ride24 platform.

§6 Partner Commission / Discount

The Partner grants Ride24 a permanent commission or discount calculated from the standard rental prices listed in the Partner account.

§7 Partner Responsibilities

The Partner is fully responsible for: the condition of the vehicles, the execution of the rental service, insurance coverage, and compliance with applicable laws.

Ride24 is not responsible for the performance of rental services by the Partner.

§8 Customer Data Protection

The Partner may receive customer personal data through the Ride24 platform. The Partner agrees to:

  • use the data only for reservation fulfillment
  • comply with GDPR regulations

§9 Reservation Documents

Customers receive access to reservation documents through their Ride24 account. Documents may include: reservation confirmation, voucher, partner rental conditions, and partner rental rules.

§10 Account Deactivation and Data Deletion

The Partner has the right to deactivate their account at any time via the panel. Upon deactivation, the Partner's offers will be immediately hidden from search results. Account data will be permanently deleted from the platform after 30 days, except for data strictly required for billing and tax purposes.
